# Logo Design Resources for ShareMyGear

If you want to get a professional logo designed, here are the best options:

## üé® Logo Design Tools (DIY)

1. **Canva** (https://canva.com)
   - Free tier available
   - Pre-made logo templates
   - Easy to customize
   - Export as SVG/PNG

2. **Figma** (https://figma.com)
   - Free for personal use
   - Professional design tool
   - Great for custom logos
   - Export as SVG

3. **LogoMaker** (https://logomaker.com)
   - Free logo generator
   - Quick and easy
   - Good for simple logos

4. **Looka** (https://looka.com)
   - AI-powered logo generator
   - Free to try, paid for downloads
   - Professional results

## üíº Professional Design Services

1. **Fiverr** (https://fiverr.com)
   - Search "logo design"
   - Prices: $5-$100+
   - Many designers available
   - Quick turnaround

2. **99designs** (https://99designs.com)
   - Logo design contests
   - Professional designers
   - Prices: $299-$1299
   - Multiple concepts

3. **Dribbble** (https://dribbble.com)
   - Find freelance designers
   - Browse portfolios
   - Contact directly
   - Prices vary

4. **Upwork** (https://upwork.com)
   - Hire freelance designers
   - Hourly or fixed price
   - Review portfolios first

## üìù Current Logo Setup

Your logo is currently in: `components/Logo.tsx`

To replace it:
1. Get/create your logo file (SVG preferred)
2. Save it to `/public/logo.svg` or `/public/logo.png`
3. Update `components/Logo.tsx` to use an `<Image>` component pointing to your file

Or if you get an SVG, you can paste it directly into the component.
